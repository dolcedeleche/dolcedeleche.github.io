Dolce

Dolce is a ten year old Oldenburg/Thoroughbred cross. She is an incredible jumper and loves to jump high! We are working on our dressage right now and she is really starting to pick it up. One of her favorite things is rolling in snow piles in the winter. She also loves a good sun bathe. Her favorite treat is probably either an apple or a banana.
